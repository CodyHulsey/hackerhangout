About the App

Hacker Hangout was developed to solve the first-world problem of having to decide just where you want to plop down and write some code when don't want to go to the office, but you do want to leave your house. Using our proprietary algorithm, hackers and other remote workers can pick the best place based on their preferences that day to stare at their laptops and enjoy some coffee or an adult beverage. Please enjoy our app. We hope it helps you Find Your Hacky Place.
